Sketch Your Mind

Nurture a Playful and Creative Brain

Release Date: June 15, 2025

A Proven, Visual-First System for Organizing Ideas

Most books on visual thinking focus on sketchnotes, mind maps, and diagrams—but this is not just another visual thinking book. Sketch Your Mind bridges two worlds: the power of visual thinking with the structured efficiency of Personal Knowledge Management (PKM). Instead of scattered visual notes, you’ll learn how to build an integrated, visual-first knowledge system—one that is creative, efficient, and deeply connected.

Inside This Book, You’ll Discover:

  • Why visuals should be first-class citizens in your knowledge system—not just decorations.
  • How to build a dynamic, reusable visual structure instead of isolated mind maps or sketchnotes.
  • Practical frameworks like Mind Maps, Hybrid Notes, The Idea Atlas, and Visual Zettelkasten.
  • How to become an ambidextrous thinker, integrating text-based and visual-first approaches.
  • The science behind spatial memory, pattern recognition, and creative iteration.

Why Read This Book?

Written by Zsolt Viczián, creator of Obsidian-Excalidraw and host of Zsolt’s Visual PKM YouTube Channel, this book isn’t just theory—it’s built on over 25 years of real-world experimentation, teaching, and tool-building.

Who Is This Book For?

Text-First Thinkers: looking to incorporate visuals without losing organization and retrieval.

Visual Creators: who want to move beyond scattered sketches and build a structured knowledge system.

Would-Be Visual Thinkers: who believe they "can’t draw" but want an intuitive way to develop and structure their ideas.
