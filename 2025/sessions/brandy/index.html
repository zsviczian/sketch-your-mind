Speaker Bio

Brandy Agerbeck

Brandy Agerbeck is a visual thinking pioneer who writes, speaks, and teaches the power of drawing as your best thinking tool. For 20+ years as a graphic facilitator she has mapped complex, real-time conversations into large-scale visual clarity.

She authored the foundational The Graphic Facilitator's Guide and followed it with The Idea Shapers, called “a new alphabet for the visual age.” Brandy’s 2013 TEDx talk Shape Your Thinking and her immersive workshop The Lab help people quiet their inner critic, organize thinking, communicate clearly, and erase overwhelm.

A “triple threat” of expert, trainer, and facilitator, she blends rigor and encouragement to make sophisticated skills immediately usable. Based in Chicago, she also delights in travel, quilting, and spy movies.

The Idea Shapers: 24 concrete visual thinking techniques to help you organize thoughts, solve problems, and communicate ideas.
