Drawing Out Your Genius

Use Visual Thinking to Sell Ideas, Drive Buy-in, and Lead Change

Abstract

In a world of AI-polished slides, hand-drawn visuals create human connection that drives real results. Discover why successful leaders are trading decks for sketches,and how to do it with confidence.

What you'll learn

  • Run innovation sessions that spark original ideas
  • Lead sales conversations that create genuine buy-in
  • Make team meetings memorable and productive
  • Move change initiatives from resistance to momentum
  • Align stakeholders around a clear shared vision

SKETCH framework (at a glance)

  • Simplify complex ideas with three essential visual building blocks
  • Kickstart innovation with customer-centered visual storytelling
  • Engage stakeholders through collaborative drawing
  • Transform meetings with live visual thinking
  • Capture information that sticks and drives action

Speaker Bio

Lisa Rothstein

Business storyteller and New Yorker cartoonist, Lisa is a former Madison Avenue creative strategist for global brands like IBM and Hanes. A Brown University Semiotics grad and certified Design Sprint/Workshopper Master and Virtual Facilitator, she helps organizations communicate, collaborate, and get buy‑in with visual thinking. Clients include Bayer, RobRoy Industries, and Consumer Reports.
