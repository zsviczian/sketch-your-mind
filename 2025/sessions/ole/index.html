System Visualization for Systems Transformation

Visual Frameworks for Personal Learning and Change

Abstract

Transform how you learn, reflect, and act by visualizing your personal systems. This interactive session gives practical methods for mapping routines, goals, and thought processes using hand-drawn frameworks and sketches. Discover how asking visual questions guides deeper understanding and sparks actionable transformation—whether in journaling, note-taking, or big life decisions. The focus is entirely on individual growth and sensemaking, not corporate strategy.

About the Author

Ole Qvist-Sørensen

Ole Qvist-Sørensen is a Danish visual facilitator, founder of Bigger Picture, and co-author of "Visual Collaboration." With over 15 years' experience, he empowers people worldwide to harness visual thinking in their personal and professional lives—from learning and reflection to collaboration and transformation.
