About Your Host

Hi, it's great to have you here! I'm Zsolt Viczián, and I'm excited that you're exploring the Sketch Your Mind Conference.

You might know me from the Visual PKM YouTube channel, the Excalidraw plugin for Obsidian, or the Visual Thinking Workshop.

This conference is, in many ways, the event I always wished I could attend myself: a gathering of the world's most inspiring voices on visual thinking, exploring the topics that fascinate me most. My belief is simple, if I find these conversations meaningful and enjoyable, others will too.

If you'd like to keep exploring, I share ideas and tools for visual knowledge in my book Sketch Your Mind, and in my free newsletter Sketch Your Mind where each issue includes a visual canvas you can use and adapt in your own practice.
